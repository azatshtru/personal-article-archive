The above image represents a wordcloud. The size of the words in the image is determined by the number of times they appear in a given document, in this case the given document is the entire script for the Shrek trilogy.
clarcbot is a Discord bot that generates wordcloud from the given discord message.

How to use?


Step 1 -> Get clarcbot on your server!
Step 2 -> Use the bot command: $wordle _YOUR PARAGRAPH HERE_ to generate a wordcloud.

How does it work?


Step 1 ->

I take out the words from the given document and arrange them by size in a list. I take natural log of base 2 of every word size to normalize it.

Step 2 ->

I place the word on the image, then I check if its on top of another word. If it is, I move the word a little bit on the spiral path and check again. If it's not, I place the word.
