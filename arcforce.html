The simplicity of ARC, the complexity of the multiplayer experience.

PROJECT ARCFORCE is an online multiplayer game where two players 1v1 using force-based gameplay.

Simple positional single touch, high reflex, multiplayer battles in your palm.

PROJECT ARCFORCE is still in development, the full feature beta-demo will be released soon enough on the Play Store (when I decide to focus on completing it :>)
